üí° Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Seed Vault?
Seed Vault is a language archive where users can store, search, and organize words, definitions, and examples. It helps you build a safe and easy-to-use collection of language data.
Q: Can I add the same word more than once?
Yes. Seed Vault allows duplicates. This can be helpful if the same word has different meanings, pronunciations, or dialect versions.
Q: What can I store in Seed Vault?
You can store words, definitions, categories, translations, and any extra notes your project needs.
Q: What characters are supported?
Seed Vault supports UTF-8 characters, which means most languages ‚Äî including non-Latin scripts and IPA ‚Äî should work normally.
Q: How do I delete an entry?
After uploading an entry it cannot be deleted without your respective administrator's authorization.
Q: Is my data safe?
Seed Vault blocks dangerous inputs like scripts or malware-like code. Still, avoid pasting code or unknown text into the app.
Q: Why didn't my file upload work?
Your file may be:
  • Too large (max 10MB)
  • The wrong format (only images, audio, documents, and video allowed)
  • Missing required data

Check your file and try again.
